About Gennady Golovkin

Gennady Gennadyevich Golovkin (born 1982) is a Kazakhstani former professional boxer. He is widely considered one of the greatest middleweight boxers of all time.

Key Dates

  1. 1982 — Born in Karaganda, Kazakhstan
  2. 2003 — Won silver medal at World Championships
  3. 2004 — Olympic silver medalist (Athens)
  4. 2010 — WBA middleweight champion
  5. 2022 — Retired from boxing

Achievements

  • Unified middleweight champion
  • Record: 42 wins (37 by KO), 2 losses, 1 draw
  • Known for knockout power and iron chin
  • One of the most terrifying and unforgettable ring announcements in the history of boxing was the introduction of Gennady ‘Triple G’ Golovkin.
  • Ladies and gentlemen, from the City of Karaganda, Kazakhstan… Here is the man who strikes fear across the middleweight division! Undefeated… with a professional record of 35 fights… 35 victories… 32 of those wins coming by way of knockout! For 8 straight years, delivering 22 consecutive knockouts! He is the reigning, defending, middleweight champion of the world… The pride of Kazakhstan… Gennady… GGG… Golovkin!!!
